Best Movies According to Joao

Top 3 Movies of All Time

Lion King

The Lion King is one of the best movies ever made. There's a reason why it has spawned three more movies.

Inception

Inception is one of my favorite movies. The amazing cinematography combined with captivating dream stories made me want to experience lucid dreaming while sleeping.

Titanic

Titanic is the best love story ever told, but it also carries the tragedy of many lives lost, which makes it incredibly poignant to know that it was based on a real event.
